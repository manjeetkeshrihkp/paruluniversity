Cybersecurity & Emerging Trends

FOC | BTech Semester 1 • PRO Animated Notes

1️⃣ Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting computer systems, networks, and digital data from unauthorized access, cyber attacks, and damage. It ensures data confidentiality, integrity, and availability.

Firewall

Monitors and filters incoming and outgoing network traffic.

Encryption

Transforms readable data into secure, unreadable ciphertext.

Malware

Malicious software such as viruses, worms, trojans, ransomware.

Phishing

Fraudulent attempts to steal sensitive information.

Cybersecurity focuses on the CIA Triad: Confidentiality, Integrity, Availability.

2️⃣ Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning

Artificial Intelligence enables machines to mimic human intelligence. Machine Learning allows systems to learn from data and improve automatically.

Data Training Model Prediction Learning

AI & ML are used in recommendation systems, fraud detection, medical diagnosis, self-driving vehicles, and smart assistants.

3️⃣ Big Data & Data Science

Big Data refers to extremely large datasets that cannot be processed using traditional methods. Data Science extracts meaningful insights from data.

  • Volume: Massive data size
  • Velocity: High data generation speed
  • Variety: Structured & unstructured data
Data Science = Statistics + Programming + Domain Knowledge

4️⃣ Future Trends in Computing

Emerging technologies are shaping the future of computing by improving speed, security, decentralization, and intelligence.

Quantum Computing

Uses qubits for massive parallel computation.

Blockchain

Decentralized and tamper-proof digital ledger.

Edge Computing

Processes data closer to the source for low latency.
